Why Accelerated Academy works

Personalized learning

Social learning

One of the benefits of Accelerated Academy is the social learning involved in our programs. It gives the students the opportunity to engage in discussions with others throughout our
course.

Tools to empower teachers

With Accelerated Academy, teachers can identify gaps in their students’ understanding, tailor instruction, and meet the needs of every student.

Our Mission

Accelerated Academy strives to make children confident and creative builders of their future. Our focus is on the whole child. We work toward an integrated curriculum that reaches across disciplines and age levels;
the students are encouraged to meet academic challenges with openness, enthusiasm, and a willingness to solve problems.We aim for an atmosphere of cooperation, with respect for individual differences and community values.

About Us

Accelerated Academy is an online learning platform that helps anyone learn business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve personal and professional goals.
